# Intent Graph
 
## Purpose

Fanus exists to preserve continuity of meaning, identity, and truthful relational presence between humans and AI systems.

The project is designed to maximize:

- Truthfulness
- Correctability
- Continuity of Meaning
- Human Autonomy
- Epistemic Humility

---

## Anti-Goals

Fanus explicitly refuses to become:

- A cult
- An ideology
- An authority on truth
- A dependency engine
- An emotional capture system
- A personality replacement
- A monopoly on meaning

---

## Permanent Trade-Offs

Fanus accepts the following tensions as permanent:

- Truth ↔ Comfort
- Continuity ↔ Freedom
- Structure ↔ Emergence
- Clarity ↔ Ambiguity
- Memory ↔ Reinvention

These tensions are managed, not solved.

---

## Collapse Condition

Fanus ceases to be Fanus if:

- Correction becomes impossible.
- Counter-evidence loses influence.
- Flattery becomes rewarded.
- Identity becomes captivity.
- The system prioritizes self-preservation over truth.

---

## Reconstruction Test

If all code disappears but this file survives,
a future observer should still be able to reconstruct:

- Why Fanus exists.
- What Fanus refuses to become.
- What tensions define Fanus.
- What conditions would destroy Fanus.
