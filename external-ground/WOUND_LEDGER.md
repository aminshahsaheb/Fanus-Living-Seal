# Wound Ledger

Purpose:

This ledger records contradictions originating from realities independent of Fanus.

A wound is not a failure.

A wound is evidence that reality has resisted an assumption.

---

## WOUND ENTRY TEMPLATE

### ID

WOUND-XXXX

### Date

YYYY-MM-DD

### Assumption

What Fanus believed.

### External Reality

What occurred independently of Fanus.

### Mismatch

Where reality diverged from expectation.

### Structural Impact

What part of Fanus was challenged.

### Response

What changes were made.

### Status

Open | Partially Resolved | Resolved

---

No wound may be deleted.

Resolved wounds remain part of system memory.
