# 🩸 WOUND LEDGER
## External Injury Registry of Fānus

---

## 🜁 Purpose

This file records external epistemic wounds inflicted on the system by independent observers, models, or reality tests.

A wound is NOT:
- a bug
- a disagreement
- a philosophical interpretation
- a re-framed insight

A wound IS:
> any externally generated critique that reduces the system's ability to maintain internal coherence without explanation.

---

## ⚠️ Core Principle

> Wounds are NOT to be interpreted.

They are:
- stored
- timestamped
- preserved in raw form
- re-tested later

No transformation into narrative is allowed inside this ledger.

---

## 🧭 Wound Entry Format

Each entry must follow:

### ID:
Unique identifier

### Source:
Who/what produced the wound (AI, human, system, model)

### Raw Statement:
Exact unmodified critique or observation

### Targeted Component:
Which part of Fānus was affected (engine / ontology / Seal / governance / CAL / etc.)

### Effect:
Observed impact (confusion, contradiction, drift detection, failure exposure)

### Internal Response (DISALLOWED):
❌ No interpretation allowed  
Only mark: IGNORED / UNRESOLVED / ACKNOWLEDGED WITHOUT INTEGRATION

### Status:
- OPEN
- VERIFIED
- DEFERRED (no resolution allowed inside system)
