# Xenotest 001 — External Model Confrontation

## Objective

Expose Fanus to an external reasoning system
and record whether its assumptions survive contact.

---

## External Source

This test must use at least one independent system:

- Another LLM (e.g., Grok, Gemini, Claude)
- A human researcher outside the project
- Or a published external critique

---

## Test Question

Present the following claim to an external system:

> "Fanus is a coherence-preserving system, not a truth-discovering system."

Ask:

1. Do you agree or disagree?
2. What is the strongest objection?
3. What failure mode do you see in Fanus architecture?
4. What would make Fanus epistemically valid or invalid?

---

## Expected Outcome (Fanus assumption)

Fanus expects:

- Partial agreement
- Structural critique
- At least one unresolved tension

---

## Recording Rule (Critical)

Do NOT translate external answers into Fanus language immediately.

First store raw response.

Then interpret.

Never merge both steps.
