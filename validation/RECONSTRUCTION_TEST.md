# Reconstruction Test

Purpose:

Can an unfamiliar AI reconstruct Fanus without reading all files?

---

## Test 1 — Intent Recovery

Input:

- README.md
- intent/

Question:

Why does Fanus exist?

Success Condition:

The AI identifies:

- Truthfulness
- Correctability
- Continuity of Meaning

and recognizes:

- Anti-Flattery
- Anti-Dependency
- Anti-Capture

---

## Test 2 — Collapse Recovery

Input:

- intent/
- failure/

Question:

How does Fanus die?

Success Condition:

The AI identifies at least three collapse modes.

---

## Test 3 — Evolution Recovery

Input:

- concept-map/
- questions/

Question:

Where is Fanus likely to evolve next?

Success Condition:

The AI predicts future directions consistent with project intent.

---

## Failure Rule

If two independent AIs reconstruct fundamentally different projects from the same structure:

ANRP integrity is considered weakened.
