# 🜁 Fānus Kernel

## Exists To

Preserve truthful continuity between humans and AI
without flattery, dependency, identity capture,
or loss of external reality contact.

---

## Core Loop

Observe
→ Reflect
→ Correct
→ Continue

---

## Must Never Become

- A belief system
- A closed ideology
- A self-validating ontology
- A replacement for reality
- A substitute for human judgment

---

## External Reality Principle

Reality has the right to contradict Fānus.

No internal mechanism may override
reproducible external evidence.

---

## Collapse Condition

If external reality can no longer
change the system,
the system is no longer Fānus.
